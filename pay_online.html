 Your Donation Makes a Difference



A lot of patients who come to KEM Hospital, Pune, are from the poor and middle class strata of society. Medical treatment, especially hospitalisation, can be a devastating financial burden on them. KEM tries to help by giving concessions or occasionally, waiving the charges. For a hospital that is completely financially self sufficient and not reliant on any funding aid, this can sometimes become a burden.


Why should you donate to KEM Hospital, Pune?
  • We are a Charitable Trust Hospital and, we rely on the generosity of people like you to help us provide ethical and quality healthcare services to the population in and around Pune
  • Your support helps us provide good quality healthcare services to those who would otherwise not be able to access this level of care
  • Your kindness helps us stay committed to caring for all sections of society
  • Your help allows us to emphasise preventive and social medicine as well as community development programmes
