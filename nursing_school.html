NURSING SCHOOL



Home >> Nursing School

The Nursing School at KEM Hospital Pune, was established in 1954. Students are trained for the Revised General Nursing and Midwifery Course, prepared to fit into the framework of Vocational Education in the 10+2+3 years programme. The Nursing School boasts of 100% results since its inception. As a teaching hospital, KEMH Pune endeavours to provide student doctors and nurses the best of opportunities to learn, to practice and to become proficient in their chosen profession.

Our Philosophy

The faculty of the KEM Hospital School of Nursing believes that the basic purpose of Nursing Education is to train students who, on completion of the education programme are able to plan and give comprehensive nursing care and health guidance to patients and their family members. The nurse must, therefore, be so trained that he/she is intellectually and morally enlightened and technically proficient. The nurse needs to be competent in teaching, oriented to community health and research minded. It is important that the nurse has the necessary knowledge, basic skills and attitude essential for professional nursing practice.


We ensure that the students acquire all these during their training.

Our Aim

The School prepares students for the ‘A’ grade Diploma Examination in Nursing and Midwifery conducted by the Maharashtra Nursing Council, Mumbai and Maharashtra State Board of Nursing & Paramedical Education.

Our Objectives

  • To enable development of skills in the use of problem solving method in nursina
  • To impart knowledge of the health resources in the community
  • To develop interpersonal relationships
  • To provide comprehensive health care nursing for the welfare of patients, families and the community
  • To contribute to the professional and personality development of the student nurses
  • To instil a positive attitude, self-awareness, self-direction and self-evaluation in the student nurses

    •  Course Details

    Course information
  • Name of the Course: Revised General Nursing and Midwifery Course – recognised by the INC, MNC & MSBNPE
  • Duration: 3 Years
  • Number of Seats: 40
  • Date of Admission: 1st August
    • Course outline

    The Syllabus and the General Nursing Course outline, as prescribed by the Indian Nursing Council/Maharashtra Nursing Council, will be followed in the school. Classroom and Clinical lectures will be given by Nursing Tutors, Clinical Instructors, and Doctors. Lectures on specialties will be given by specialists in the field. Students are assigned hospital duties for clinical experience in various departments, such as Medicine, Surgery, Paediatrics, Gynaecology & Obstetrics, Operation Theatre, Community and Family Welfare Department, as a part of their training.

    Clinical Explosure

    KEM Hospital Pune, has excellent clinical facilities with 550+ beds in the General Wards, which include male and female Medical and Surgical wards, Paediatric, Obstetrics and Gynaecology wards. In addition, there are Operation Theatres and Intensive Care Units. Students are also sent to the following Centres:

  • Kamala Nehru Hospital & Sonawane Hospital for obstetric experience
  • Nityanand Mental Health Care Centre, Katraj for psychiatric experience
    • Community Nursing Experience
  • Rural Health at KEM’s Vadu Hospital and at the PHC at Wagholi
  • Urban Health at the Urban Health Centre, Bhawani Peth, Pune.

    •  Admission & Selection

    Criteria for Selection

    Ad.Proceddure

    Diploma

    Notes

  • H.S.C. (10+2 passed) from Maharashtra State with Marathi as one of the subjects. Preference will be given to Science (Physics, Chemistry & Biology) subjects with an aggregate of 45% marks
  • Age between 17 years completed to 25 years at the time of admission
  • Admission is done purely on merit: 50% of H.S.C. marks, number of attempts plus performance in personal interview and written test
  • Medical Fitness:
  • Candidates must be mentally and physically fit
  • Students have to undergo a medical examination at the KEM Hospital at their own cost
  • They will be considered for selection only after obtaining a Medical Fitness Certificate
